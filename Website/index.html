The Cheese Game

instructions : how to play

This Game comes as a simple game with simple and not many rules.

The Cheesegame, also known as Dots and Boxes, is a game which is played by two players. It begins with an empty grid of dots. Players must take turns in connecting the dots to form a box, with a horizontal or a vertical line between two unconnected dots. The player who completes a 1 x 1 box receives a point and takes another turn. Once a player has completed a box, a cross will appear inside the completed box with the color the player used to draw the lines. The game finishes when there are no more lines to be set and the player with the most points wins.
